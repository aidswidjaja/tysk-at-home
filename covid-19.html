Latest NSW Government update dated 20 August 2021 Latest NSW Department of Education update dated 27 August 2021

Greater Sydney stay-at-home order

  • The stay at home order has been extended until the end of September
  • You can only shop or exercise 5km from home
  • Exercise is limited to 1 hour per day for the 12 LGAs of concern
  • For the 12 LGAs of concerns, additional restrictions apply, including 9pm to 5am curfew
  • Pfizer vaccines are available for those aged 16-39 in LGAs of concern
  • Vaccines will roll out to the entire 16-39 age group by the end of August

NSW School Updates

All of Greater Sydney is currently engaged in remote learning. HSC exams have been delayed until 9 November.

Level 3

Full return for areas which are no longer under lockdown

  • Full return to school
  • Reduced mingling and on-site activities

Level 3 Plus

Staged return for areas still under stay-at-home orders

  • Year 11 students who are not in areas of concern will return from 1 November
  • Year 12 students who are not in areas of concern will return full-time from 25 October
  • Details for other year groups are available on the NSW Department of Education website
  • Face coverings required for indoor and outdoor settings for staff and students
  • No mingling or on-site activities

Level 4

LGAs and suburbs of concerns under Level 4 restrictions will remain learning from home

SAC Updates

  • Remote learning continues via Microsoft Teams and Canvas (or Zoom for co-curricular music)
