Latest NSW Government update

Latest NSW Government update dated 20 August 2021

Greater Sydney stay-at-home order

  • The stay at home order has been extended until the end of September
  • You can only shop or exercise 5km from home
  • Exercise is limited to 1 hour per day for the 12 LGAs of concern
  • For the 12 LGAs of concerns, additional restrictions apply, including 9pm to 5am curfew
  • Pfizer vaccines are available for those aged 16-39 in LGAs of concern
  • Vaccines will roll out to the entire 16-39 age group by the end of August

NSW School Updates

  • All of Greater Sydney is engaged in remote learning
  • Year 12 HSC students will no longer be back by August 16 for physical learning
  • "All exams and other education ... will move online, not including the HSC. The government will provide further information on its education plan in due course" (NSW Government, 20 August 2021)

SAC Updates

  • Remote learning continues via Microsoft Teams and Canvas (or Zoom for co-curricular music)
