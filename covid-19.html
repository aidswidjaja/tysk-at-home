Greater Sydney stay-at-home order

  • The stay at home order has been extended until the end of September
  • You can only shop or exercise 5km from home
  • Exercise is no longer limited to 1 hour per day for the 12 LGAs of concern
  • Restrictions are being standardised across Greater Sydney
  • Pfizer vaccines are available for those aged 16-39 in LGAs of concern
  • Vaccines will roll out to the entire 12-39 like, now!

NSW School Updates

All of Greater Sydney is currently engaged in remote learning. HSC exams have been delayed until 9 November.

  • HSC students can return to school full-time from 18 October
  • Class of 2022 will hopefully return to school on 25 October
    • HSC exams delayed until 9 November
  • Details for other year groups are available on the NSW Department of Education website
  • Face coverings required for indoor and outdoor settings for staff and students
  • No mingling or on-site activities
    • More info from the NSW Department of Education
